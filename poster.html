EcoGuardian is a state-of-the-art environmental monitoring platform that bridges the gap between concerned citizens, administrative oversight, and field authorities. Leveraging real-time synchronization via Socket.io and Gemini 1.5 Flash AI, every environmental threat — from illegal logging to wildlife injuries — is tracked, triaged, and resolved with total transparency.

Problem & Solution
  • ❌ Environmental incidents go unnoticed due to fragmented systems
  • ❌ Citizens lack a transparent channel to verify complaint resolution
  • ❌ Authorities struggle to triage real vs. fake/spam reports
  • ❌ No unified real-time coordination between all stakeholders
  • ✅ Unified platform with role-based dashboards for all parties
  • ✅ End-to-end transparent timeline from submission to resolution
  • ✅ Gemini AI automatically flags suspicious and fake reports
  • ✅ Live Socket.io push notifications keep everyone in sync

Tri-Role Architecture
👤

Citizen (User)

  • Submit & track environmental reports
  • Upload photo evidence with AI scan
  • Earn points · View leaderboard
🔧

Command (Admin)

  • AI authenticity verification of all reports
  • Assign incidents to field authority
  • Manage alerts & broadcast announcements
🚨

Field Authority

  • Accept & investigate assigned cases
  • Upload photo-of-resolution as proof
  • Real-time chat coordination
